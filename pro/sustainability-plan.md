# Sustainability Plan

## Funding Model
- Micro-subscriptions: 1-5 Ghana Cedis per month via Mobile Money (MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money).
- Freemium model: Basic modules free, advanced content premium.

## Partnerships
- Telecom companies: MTN, Telecel for data bundles and promotion.
- NGOs: Collaboration with digital literacy NGOs for funding.
- Corporate sponsors: Companies like Vodafone for CSR initiatives.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription fees
- Sponsored content from tech companies
- Merchandise (Adinkra-themed tech accessories)
- Training workshops for businesses

## Long-term Goals
- Self-sustaining within 2 years
- Expand to other African countries
- Integrate with government digital literacy programs
